About Us

Welcome to the NoCalifonies movement. Depending where you are, the brand can take on many different meanings. Whether you’re from California and tired of our state being taken over by the clueless, money grubbing politicians and folks that move here with their entitled, privileged attitudes or from another state and have a distaste of the very same thing. We at the NoCalifonies World HQ were all born and raised here. The memories are fond of the state we grew up in. We want to welcome everyone to experience the beauty of this great state we are so proud of. This invitation however, has a disclaimer: “You must be a positive influence while you’re here!

ie: Don’t leave your trash and debris on our beaches and in our parks. Don’t disrespect our beautiful state, take pride in having the privilege to be here. Be respectful to those who have made California the Great State it is! And finally enjoy your visit, just please don’t move here. If you have moved here because of work or school, you now have an obligation to the state you now call home. In an effort to self-police, we offer you the ability to wear the brand. Hats, T-Shirts and stickers for everything from your bumper to skateboards. Everyone will know where you stand. People are going to ask you, “What’s a NoCalifonie”? Trust me, it happens to us on a daily basis. When asked, my general response is, “If you have to ask you might just be one”! We’re not saying you have to like it. This may even be a bit politically incorrect. So, if your offended by anything you’ve read here, don’t worry, pack your trash and just move on!

Thanks for stopping by and have a great day!
